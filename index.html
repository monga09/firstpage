Complete Cyber Security

WhiteHaX CyberSafe Alpha employs the industry-first ground-up design to protect against ransomware & malware infections, WiFi intrusions, web risks, ID-theft and many other potential threats specifically targeting homes and small and medium size businesses.

Measure risk from within Insured's Firewall

Periodic, Uniform way to get visibility in to risk associated with every insured on the portfolio — not just once but through-out the coverage period. At the same time help Insured improve their cyber security posture.

Prospect's Risk Evaluation

Provide prospects a free pen-testing service while getting visibility in to their cyber readiness before underwriting the policy.

WhiteHaX Betterment Service

After a cyber incident or breach, help insured have continuous visibility in to their cyber readiness through a thorough verification of their deployed control as well as endpoint and server vulnerabilities.

Cloud-hosted External Servers

Enforce no-install, no-impact monitoring on all cloud-hosted servers with WhiteHaX hunter. With simple, hand-off management and no performance impact, WhiteHaX Hunter is an ideal solution for IoC hunting on externally visible servers whether they are virtual, container-based or physical servers.

Internal Application Servers

Use cloud-hosted WhiteHaX Hunter for early detection of security exploits or other malicious attempts before they turn in to full-fledge breaches.

Security Monitoring or Enforcement Servers

Internet servers like DMZ servers and Security monitoring or enforcement devices like Web Proxies, SNORT IPS and other network/application monitoring servers are critical to multi-level defenses of an Enterprise. Utilize WhiteHaX Hunter to immediately detect intrusion attempts before they become a breach.
