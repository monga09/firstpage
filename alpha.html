Cyber Risks for Home & Businesses:

While last decade has seen unprecedented increase in cyber attacks on large businesses, in last 3 years cyber attacks on individuals, home and small-medium business (SMB) networks have also exponentially increased. In fact, in 2019-2020 one in every ten small business has been a target of cyber attacks primarily Ransomware and Malware infections through Phishing.

In order to provide better risk control and reduce pay-out risks for cyber Insurers in personal-lines & SMB market, IronSDN has developed a low-cost, plug-and-protect appliance called WhiteHaX Alpha, to protect and prevent home owners and SMBs from falling prey to various cyber breaches. WhiteHaX Alpha is specifically developed for home and small business networks to provide industrial-strength cyber security with the ease-of-use and simplicity required by home & SMB users.

WhiteHaX CyberSafe Alpha employs the industry-first ground-up design to protect against ransomware & malware infections, WiFi intrusions, web risks, ID-theft and many other potential threats specifically targeting homes and small and medium size businesses.

Zero Configuration, Under 2-min Install

WhiteHaX Alpha is a true plug-and-protect device. Simply plug it into either the home/business WiFi or Ethernet port on the network router and its up-and-running. Unlike firewall and other cyber security devices, there is no complicated configuration to set up. In fact, it requires no configuration at-all except providing WiFi password and registering the user. Unlike Anti-virus and other endpoint security solutions, there is no need to install it on every machine. It takes less than 2-minute to install and as soon as its up, it starts protecting the business.

Industrial-strength Cyber Protection against Home/SMB specific Threats :

Large businesses and Enterprises invest millions of dollars and have team full of professionals for cyber security. Home owners and SMBs have minimal resources, yet now-a-days they need similarly deep cyber protection. While ransomware and malware infections are primary threats, they are also prone to malicious websites, WiFi Intrusions, ID-theft and other Data-and-Privacy Risks. WhiteHaX Alpha detects and prevents such threats and many more from harming home and small business owners.

Worlds Most Used Business IPS, now available for Home & SMBs:

: WhiteHaX Alpha includes the most deployed open-source intrusion prevention system (IPS), called SNORT, which has over a million business deployments. SNORT has been ported in WhiteHaX Alpha and customized for home and SMB use. SNORT IPS detects thousands of known cyber attacks, breach scenarios and other malicious activities, which in turn helps WhiteHaX Alpha prevent such cyber attacks from targeting home users and home or business WiFi. .

Active Prevention of Ransomware and Malware Infections

WhiteHaX Alpha is the only home device which includes a threat-intelligence-based Malware & Phishing Filter which eliminates access to any Ransomware, Malware, BOTNET or other malicious sites. It is based on Advanced Threat Intelligence collected from over 100 sources such as Govt. Agencies, Security Organizations, big businesses and large cyber-security vendors accumulated in to known malicious websites list. The list is updated at the WhiteHaX cloud server every 5 mins. On an average the Advanced Threat Intelligence identifies tens-of-thousands of IP addresses (typical average of 80,000+ websites) to eliminate any malware or phishing threat to home or SMB device. It is always ON by default to automatically protect all Home and Business connected devices from such cyber threats.

Transparent Firewall

Typical cyber security firewalls require two network connections to enforce traffic blocking rules. WhiteHaX Alpha includes an intelligent, transparent firewall which can detect and enforce traffic rules from any device connected on the home or business network to the Internet. The built-in firewall is optimized to either allow manual traffic blocking rules or potentially risky or malicious website access detected from a home or business device to an external site. With a simple click, the user can create and enforce various types of firewall rules to block access to bad sites, IPs, applications, category of sites and more.

Smart Network Scan with WiFi Intrusion Detection

Smart devices in home or SMB network such as Point of Sale devices, smart Doorbells, Thermostats, TVs, cameras etc. are a constant target of hackers. WhiteHaX Alpha performs a network scan of all connected smart devices and identifies their potential cyber risks. WhiteHaX Alpha also immediately detects when a new device connects to the business WiFi or a hacker tries to breach WiFi security. It then immediately alerts of a potential intrusion and provides a one-click response to block the intruder permanently.

Works in Conjunction with WhiteHaX CyberSafe Apps

WhiteHaX CyberSafe Mobile Apps are counterparts to WhiteHaX Alpha. While Alpha provides on-premise protection for the entire home or business network, the mobile apps provide on-the-go security for personal/mobile devices of home users or the business (employees) such as laptops, desktops, smart phones, tablets, MACs etc. Using WhiteHaX Alpha in conjunction with WhiteHaX mobile Apps, provides industry-first on-premise and on-the-go security for Home and SMBs.

Small Price, Big Features

Starting at low cost of $299, WhiteHaX Alpha offers the same cyber security features a large Enterprise would expect, with the ease-of-use and simplicity required for the Home & SMB owners. All features of WhiteHaX Alpha are available through the Alpha mobile App. Simply download it on the mobile phone and the entire home or business security is at your fingertip. The WhiteHaX Alpha App interactively shows alerts for any new device connection, malicious or inappropriate website access or alerts from either SNORT IPS or the Threat Intelligence based Malware/Phishing Filter. Users can then take action to set one-time or permanent actions to block such traffic, if not already blocked by Alpha automatically.
